Royal Computers & Communications

Established and led by Mohammed Mansur Ahmed Khan, Royal Computers & Communications is a premium IT sales & services hub located in Rajamahendravaram, East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh.

For over 10 years, we have provided Rajamahendravaram with premium-grade desktops, laptops, multi-brand printers, and network installations. Built on a foundation of technical honesty and fast corporate support, we have grown to serve over 5,000 satisfied clients.

To establish Royal Computers as the ultimate technical partner in Andhra Pradesh, where absolute quality meets technological innovation, delivering computing solutions with a premium luxury service experience.

We commit to supplying 100% genuine components, executing expert hardware diagnostics with absolute integrity, maintaining rapid-support cycles, and designing custom installations that ensure business continuity.
